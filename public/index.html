In chrome dev channel, Android

  • Step 1: Go offline.
  • Step 2: Register BG Sync from above register button.
  • Step 3: Enter a git username and click add.
  • Step 4: Go online and sync will be triggered when user is gets connectivity and card will be updated.

or Emulate via chrome devTools

  • Step 2: Go to application tab in chrome canary devTools, navigate to serviceWorker tab.
  • Step 3: Go online and click `sync` button to emulate sync.
  • Step 4: Above card will be updated as per your input.
